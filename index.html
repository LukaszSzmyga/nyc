New York City is great!

New York City is often referred to collectively as the five boroughs:

  1. Manhattan
  2. Brooklyn
  3. Queens
  4. Bronx
  5. Staten Island

In New York City you can see many attractions, such as

  • Central Park
  • Statue of Liberty
  • Empire State Building
  • Times Square
  • Rockefeller Center

and much more.
